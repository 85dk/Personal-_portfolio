Cuisine Cloud: AI-Powered Recipe Generating App

Cuisine Cloud is an intelligent, cross-platform mobile and web application designed to revolutionize the way people cook by using the power of artificial intelligence. This innovative app enables users to generate customized recipes based on the ingredients they already have at home, significantly reducing food waste and making meal planning more spontaneous and efficient. Developed using Flutter and integrated with Firebase and Gemini 2.0 Flash AI, the app delivers a smooth, secure, and responsive user experience across Android, iOS, and web platforms. It offers core features such as ingredient-based recipe generation, recipe publishing, favorites management, and personalized suggestions. Users can not only receive dynamic meal ideas but also contribute their own culinary creations to a global community. Unlike traditional apps like Cookpad or Yummly that rely on static databases, Cuisine Cloud adapts to individual preferences in real-time, empowering users to explore global flavors and improve their cooking confidence.
